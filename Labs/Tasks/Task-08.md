## Task 08 - Reports & Metrics

### Objective

Analyze project performance using Jira's built-in reports.

---

??? question "Task 1: Sprint Analysis"

    1. Open the Sprint Report for your latest completed sprint
    2. Document:
        - Committed story points
        - Completed story points
        - Completion rate (%)
        - Number of issues added/removed mid-sprint
    3. Open the Burndown Chart
    4. Identify and describe at least 2 patterns you observe

??? question "Task 2: Velocity Tracking"

    1. Open the Velocity Chart
    2. If you have data for multiple sprints:
        - Calculate the average velocity
        - Identify the trend (increasing/decreasing/stable)
        - Recommend a target for the next sprint
    3. If single sprint: calculate what your velocity would be and explain how you'd use it for planning

??? question "Task 3: Executive Dashboard"

    Build an Executive Dashboard with at least 5 gadgets:

    1. Sprint Health / Burndown
    2. Velocity Chart
    3. Pie Chart (by Priority)
    4. Created vs Resolved chart (last 30 days)
    5. Filter Results showing overdue items
    6. Take a screenshot and write a 3-sentence executive summary
