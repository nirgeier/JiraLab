## Task 04 - Workflow Basics

### Objective

Practice transitioning issues through their complete lifecycle.

---

??? question "Task 1: Full Lifecycle Walkthrough"

    Take 3 issues from your backlog and walk each through the complete workflow:

    1. **Issue A**: Transition using **drag & drop** on the Board
        - To Do → In Progress → Done
    2. **Issue B**: Transition using the **Status button** on the issue detail
        - To Do → In Progress → In Review → Done
    3. **Issue C**: Use **bulk transition**
        - Select 3 issues and move them all to "In Progress"

    Document each transition and note the time it took.

??? question "Task 2: Workflow Investigation"

    1. Open any issue and click **View Workflow**
    2. Document:
        - All status names
        - All transitions (from → to)
        - Which statuses allow backward transitions?
    3. Draw a simple diagram of the workflow (use pen and paper or a tool)

??? question "Task 3: Resolution and Reopen"

    1. Move an issue to **Done**
    2. Check the **Resolution** field — what value was set?
    3. **Reopen** the issue (transition back to In Progress or To Do)
    4. Check the Resolution field again — was it cleared?
    5. Document your findings
