## Task 07 - Team Management

### Objective

Practice managing a team's workload and planning capacity.

---

??? question "Task 1: Workload Distribution"

    1. Create 12 issues across 3 Epics
    2. Assign issues to 3 different assignees (4 each)
    3. Ensure each person has a mix of priorities (High, Medium, Low)
    4. Set story points on all issues
    5. Verify balanced workload: no assignee has more than 40% of total points
    6. Use a Board with Swimlanes by Assignee to visualize

??? question "Task 2: Capacity Planning"

    1. Calculate team capacity for a 2-week sprint:
        - Assume 3 developers, 6 productive hours/day, 80% availability
        - Convert to story points (assume 1 point ≈ 4 hours)
        - Expected capacity = ?
    2. Plan a sprint that matches this capacity
    3. Document: Total points planned vs. capacity
    4. If over/under, adjust the sprint scope

??? question "Task 3: Progress Monitoring Dashboard"

    Create a "Manager Dashboard" with:

    1. **Workload Pie Chart** — group by Assignee
    2. **Sprint Burndown** — current sprint
    3. **Filter Results** — blocked or stale issues (use JQL)
    4. **Two Dimensional Filter** — Rows: Assignee, Columns: Status
    5. Take a screenshot of the completed dashboard
